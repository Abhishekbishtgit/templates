What is Shipping Template?
A feature of the “Wish Integration” app that differentiates it from any other apps in the market. The Shipping templates are pre-decided shipping information that you can assign to products while listing on Wish. The Shipping templates include information related to the shipping origin, the time period to process the order, the shipping cost, etc. This information is then uploaded along with the products while listing on Wish Marketplace.
Depending on the nature of the products you can create multiple shipping templates. It ensures smooth updates of products on the Wish marketplace.
