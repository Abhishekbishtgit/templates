Differentiate your products With customized Order notes and tags for seamless selling
Are you tired of rechecking product details? Need a more straightforward process to distinguish products for shipping? Worry no more!

CedCommerce's Walmart Shopify integration brings you the all-new Order Notes and Order Tags features. The new order notes feature enables you to easily add special instructions or other information for ease of shipping products. On the other hand, Order tags assist you in assigning customized tags to your product orders. They distinguish different rules and criteria based on what's included in order. For example, you can specify whether an order is a high priority, fragile, backorder, requires insurance, or fraud risk with order tags.

Go to Settings> Order Configuration> Add Order Notes/Order Tags
