WISH MARKETPLACE INTEGRATION
An important update on “Wish Marketplace Integration” app
Say hello to all new and updated features of CedCommerce’s “Wish Marketplace Integration app”
To provide merchants with a more delightful and streamlined selling experience, Wish recently updated to V3 API. In compliance with the same, CedCommerce modified and updated the “Wish Marketplace Integration” app that aims at providing a seamless and smooth selling experience to Wish merchants.

Bringing merchants the opportunity to sync 4x faster than before
Specifically, we refined the overall product syncing to help merchants better sync their Wish products and order with our integration app and thus save considerable time. Product sync will enhance offering by allowing merchants to sync required attributes, empowering sellers to link new products or change existing products and sync it with the Integration app.
Merchants can now experience quick and seamless product upload
In addition, bulk product upload has also become faster, to assist merchants further add multiple products to the app in one go. The prolonged process is now shortened and merchants get to experience a hassle-free product upload function.
With the update to V3 API, CedCommerce is actively working to redesign your selling experience and make every day better and easier for you.
