Here are our two new App updates that we cant wait to share with you:-
#Update1. Disable Etsy Tax for all country in Shopify order
This new action is meant for sellers from all countries selling on Etsy through the app. Earlier, when sellers sync their orders from Etsy to Shopify, they need to pay taxes on both platforms. Since it was getting difficult for small and medium businesses to manage their funds or something, we have come up with the new feature update, enabling you to exclude the taxes on Shopify for Etsy Orders. Since you have already paid tax to Etsy, this setting is made to exempt Shopify taxes, i.e., excluding double taxation.
#Update2. Skip Shopify revenue calculations for Etsy orders
Once you sync the Etsy and Shopify stores through the app, it is effortless to manage the orders on both. Earlier after the completion of orders, whenever you take out the revenue report of your Shopify store, the analytics reports also included Etsy orders. But enabling this new feature, you can fetch only Shopify stores’ analytics which does not include revenue earned from Etsy orders.
